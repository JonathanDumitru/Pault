## Description

Pault is a fast, local-first prompt library for macOS.

Capture prompt ideas, organize them with tags, and launch them in seconds from the menu bar or with a global hotkey. Pault keeps your library on your Mac and gives you a focused workspace for writing, editing, and reusing high-quality prompts.

Use Pault to:
- Build and maintain a reusable prompt library.
- Organize with tags, favorites, and archives.
- Find prompts quickly with full-text search.
- Open prompts from the menu bar while you work.
- Trigger a launcher with a global hotkey for quick access.
- Copy or paste prompts into your active app in one flow.

Why teams and power users pick Pault:
- Local-first by default.
- Clean split-view editing for faster iteration.
- Designed for repeatable workflows, not one-off notes.
