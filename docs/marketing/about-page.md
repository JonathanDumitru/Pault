## Story

`Pault started from a simple problem: strong prompts were getting lost across notes apps, chat histories, and temporary docs. We wanted a faster way to keep good work close, improve it over time, and reuse it in real workflows.`

`The result is a local-first macOS app built for practical prompt operations. Capture what works, refine it, and launch it in seconds.`

## Principles

1. `Local-first workflow`
Prompt libraries should stay under the user's control.

2. `Fast retrieval`
Useful prompts are only valuable when they are easy to find at the right moment.

3. `Operational clarity`
A prompt library should feel like a reliable system, not a random folder of snippets.

## What We Are Building

`Pault is focused on becoming the home base for serious prompt workflows: better organization, cleaner launch surfaces, and dependable day-to-day reuse.`
